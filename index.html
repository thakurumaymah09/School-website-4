Campus life

library

The library plays a vital role in the lives of the students, as it develops their reading skills. The school librarian encourages the students to read books, as it helps them broaden horizons, and stimulates their thinking.

computer lab

Our school has a spacious state-of-the-art computer lab comprising of multiple computers to ensure that each student gets to work on a separate system and follow instructions on a one-to-one basis. The latest software enables them hone their creativity and designing skills, as well as develop their logical reasoning. So this process of learning simply lays the foundation for their future endeavours.

Smart Board

The interactive boards make learning more interesting and effective. The children enjoy learning, and this enhances their cognitive development. This audio-visual technology has made a great impact on their learning skills.

News-In-Education (NIE) 2018
News-In-Education (NIE) 2018

Miss Shaikh Falak Qamar, currently studying in class 10th has been awarded the Student of the Year award by Times NIE. More than 300 top-scorers from schools across Mumbai were felicitated recently during a function of the ‘Times NIE Student of the Year’ award on September 11, 2018. Times NIE in association with Yukti Foundation organised this prestigious event at Yashwant Natya Mandir in Matunga. The awards were presented to students based on their academic performance in class 9. Currently in class 10, these young achievers accepted their trophies. The event which was attended by more than 250 students and their parents, was hosted by actor, anchor and psychotherapist Maninee De Mishra. Shaikh Falak credits the teaching staff, her mentors and her parents and is full of gratitude and hopes to inspire other Anthonians, make their Alma mater proud!

Abacus
Abacus

Alina Shaikh’s nimble, fast fingers have taken her places. This time in Charni Road, where she won the Ideal Play Abacus India Pvt. Ltd. in the Level III of Elementary competition in abacus. A release from her abacus training institute, Ideal Play Abacus India Pvt. Ltd., says that she won the prize, where around 3000 students participated in various levels. Her task was to complete 100 addition and subtraction problems in five minutes. The student who gets the maximum correct answers at the shortest possible time is declared the winner, says Alina’s Sister Ms. Amreen Shaikh who has supported her in the run up to the competition. In fact, it was she who enrolled her in an abacus institute in their neighbourhood in Agripada. “I encouraged her to join an abacus coaching class after finding her do math with interest,” she recalls. This was a year ago. Ms. Allina Shaikh has been congratulated by the Principal and the staff for her brilliant performance.

Sports and games
Sports & games

“Sports breaks the weak & exposes the strong.” These iconic words rang loud, clear on 24th February 2018, when our talented students swept through gold and silver medals in the in the 5th Inter-School Karate Championship 2018 at the Mumbai University Sports Ground, Marine Line, Mumbai. The extremely fit, agile and energetic youngsters of St. Anthony did us proud.
